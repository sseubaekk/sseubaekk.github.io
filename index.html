Seungyeon Baek

I am a graduate student in the Department of Industrial Engineering and Electrical Engineering at Texas Tech University. I like to design and assessment works, products in system by using quantitative (statistical and computational) and qualitative (ethnography, human-centered design) methods to make our daily lives convenient, efficient and safe. ​

My main research interests reduce uncertainty in human-centered data, and support resilience in algorithmic information flows. Among many challenges, I mainly focus on addressing safety and quality issues to close the gap betwwen data patterns and reality. This will be achieved by examining the imbalance between prescribed tasks (WAI) and routine activities (WAD). I aim to develop simulation and test scenario generation software for multi-agents, facilitating autonomous verification. I have also actively worked on the projects related to the field of communication network systems, especially biomedical fields.
